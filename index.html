How AI Mapper works

A simple three-step flow designed for comms and analytics teams – no developer required.

1

Input

Paste a press release, blog post or owned content URL. The app pulls key structure and text.

2

Analyze

Content is evaluated against SEO and GEO lenses, scoring technical, on-page and AI-readiness cues.

3

Improve

You get dual scores plus a short list of practical edits you can brief straight into your content teams.

Try the prototype

Paste sample content and generate demo scores. Supabase is wired in so you can later save results and plug in real scoring via serverless functions.

This build does not call OpenAI yet – scores are generated with a simple heuristic so you can wire up UI, Supabase and hosting first.
